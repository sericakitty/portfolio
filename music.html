DJ Serica Kitty

I'm a Finnish DJ girl who loves to play all kinds of energetic and beautiful electronic music. My goal is to make people dance and love beautiful melodies with powerful bass.

Genres:

My main genres are J-Core, futurecore, J-Freeform, J-trance / Trance, J-Psytrance / Psytrance (fullon). I also play happy hardcore, UK hardcore, and freeform.

Familiar DJ equipments:

Pioneer CDJ 2000nxs players and newer & Pioneer DJM-mixers. I also use the Rekordbox app with Pioneer controllers. I also have some experience with Traktor DJ controllers (S4). I have also started to produce music. Stay tuned, releases coming in the future:3
